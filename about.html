Instructional Designer & Learning Experience Designer

Hi, I’m Vikas—an instructional designer and developer based in Abu Dhabi, UAE.

Originally from India, I found my way into instructional design soon after college. By 2010 I knew this was my craft: packaging knowledge into clear, usable learning that people can understand, enjoy, and apply at work and in life. I love using creativity to make learning feel simple and worth someone’s time.

I moved to the UAE in 2013 and have had the privilege of working across public and private sectors. My projects range from ILT to e-learning and blended programs, partnering end-to-end with teams to take ideas from discovery to launch.

I’m energized by how technology keeps expanding what’s possible in learning—smarter workflows, humane AI, better personalization, and accessible design that meets learners where they are. The goal stays the same: use the best tools available to build learning that looks good, works well, and creates real impact—capability, confidence, and performance.
