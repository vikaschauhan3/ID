The CMI Certification Video Guide Series was recognized with a Brandon Hall Group Bronze Award for best use of video in learning (2018).

Overview

A series of short, animated microlearning videos designed to help learners successfully navigate the CMI certification process. In this project at KGC, I partnered with a subject matter expert to translate the certification journey into clear, bingeable guidance aligned to real learner needs.

Approach

  • SME collaboration: Worked closely with the subject matter expert to translate CMI content into a well packaged, short learning series to help learners with the CMI certification.
  • Maping learning process: Broke down the CMI certification journey to identify top priority videos for learners and mapped content to certification objectivs.
  • Scripting: Scripted concise, learner-first guidance microlearning scripts for the videos.
  • Storyboarding → animation: Built storyboards and produced animated videos in Vyond, optimizing for bit-sized learning, legibility and pacing.
  • Microlearning cadence: Kept videos short and focused to aid recall and encourage completion.
  • LMS delivery: Packaged and published to the LMS for learners access.
